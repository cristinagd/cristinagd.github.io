About Me

My name is Cristina Diaz, I am a full time student at Colorado State University- Pueblo. I am currently a sophomore majoring in Mass communication and Business Marketing. I love meeting new people and building friendships. My hobbies are designing different things and watching movies. I am a team leader and strive to be the best I can be. I am a person who likes things to be fair, and I hate when people take advantage of others. In this world, no one is more than anyone. Treating people with respect is important to me.

I have lived in Pueblo, Colorado my whole life. I came from a big family. We are six sisters and two brothers. Family is very important to me as well as being humbled. When I was younger, college didn't even come across my mind. I went to highschool and thought that after school I was going to find a good job. It wasn't until my senior year that i was really focused and wanting to attend College. I started asking for help from my counselors. Once I started to know that I was going to be able to attend college when I started to get more into the whole concept of college and what I needed to do.

I am an opportunity seeker and I find doors that are going to give me opportunities. My first year at my college were the best, I met the most helpful and humble people. I was able to meet people that are a huge influence in my life as well. I've always worked hard for what I want and when I make up my mind about something, I won't stop until it gets done. This has helped me get far and achieve my goals that I set for myself.

My mom has been a huge part of my life as well, always there giving me support and being my right shoulder. She has given everything i needed to be successful and more. Everything I do is for her because I want her to be proud of me. I also do it for my family because I am the first of eight siblings to attend college. I want my nieces and nephews to have someone to look up to. I didn't have that person to lead me towards college and I want to be a leader for my family members.

Having goals is something important to me as well, goals help us have a motivation in life towards something. I have many goals, and every day I worked hard to achieve them. I believe we are all capable of anything. We are all smart, just putting our full focus and having faith in us we are able to achieve anything. If they can do it, why can't you? I have a favorite quote that my sister once told me, and it says “I'm not better than anyone, but nobody is better than me” I am me, and everything I do is to help me. I want to be the best that I can be for myself.
